## Overview

Ph Haiyan Advocacy Inc. needed a digital platform that reflects its resilience mission and supports community-facing communication.

## Core Objective

Present advocacy goals in a clear and credible format while making it easier for users to understand services, browse resources, and take action.

## Solution

The website experience emphasizes clarity, emotional context, and easy navigation for different audience types.

- Strong mission-led hero narrative
- Accessible top-level navigation for key content areas
- Action-oriented callouts for engagement and support

![Ph Haiyan Advocacy Inc. live website](/images/case-studies/ph-haiyan-live.png)

## Implementation Notes

Content sections were structured for readability on mobile and desktop, with clear content groupings for advocacy messaging, letters, forums, and contact.

## Outcome

The live platform supports public trust and community engagement by delivering a focused advocacy narrative with direct pathways for participation.
